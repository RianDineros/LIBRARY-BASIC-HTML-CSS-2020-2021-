BRAVE NEW WORLD

TITLE : BRAVE NEW WORLD


CHARACTER : BERNARD MARX

LEINA CROWNE

MUSTAPHA MOND


SUMMARY : The novel examines a futuristic society, called the World State, that revolves around science and efficiency. In this society, emotions and individuality are conditioned out of children at a young age, and there are no lasting relationships because “every one belongs to every one else” (a common World State dictum).
